WHAT WE DO ?

BMI Calculation and Assessment:

Accurately calculate the BMI (Body Mass Index) of children under 5 and assess their nutritional health based on entered biological details.

Personalized Nutritional Recommendations:

Provide tailored suggestions for nutritional food items and recipes based on the child's BMI, taking into account the availability and affordability of resources in rural areas.

Natural Alternatives for Improved Health:

Promote the use of natural alternatives and locally sourced ingredients to meet the nutritional demands of children, focusing on affordability and long-term health benefits.

Targeted Solutions for Malnutrition:

Target the specific needs of children under 5 to maintain a healthy BMI range and combat malnutrition through practical and feasible recommendations, ensuring better overall health outcomes.

We Help combat Malnutrition

We keenly focus on offering affordable and accessible meal plans in order to reduce malnutrition in children. Additionally, providing customised diet plans for the children under five years to eradicate the malnutrition especially in rural areas. This helps to incorporate healthy eating habits in them and provides them with the best possible start in life.
